## How to Enable

1. Open Settings → Desktop & Hotkeys
2. Toggle "Autostart" switch on
3. Grant authorization if prompted

## How it Works

- Starts in background, no popup
- Shows icon in system tray
- Hotkeys automatically active

## System Support

- ✅ Windows
- ✅ macOS
- ✅ Linux

## How to Disable

Turn off the switch in Blinko settings, or disable in system startup items management.
